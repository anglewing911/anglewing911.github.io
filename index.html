About Me

I'm Yitsin Liao and I'm a Product/UI Designer

I have always enjoy art and design since I was a kid, and it is also one of the many reasons I study in Arts and Technology at University of Texas at Dallas. It is my dream to be able to create magnificent design.

Born in Taiwan and immigrate to United States with my family when I was 10 years old. I grow up in the States and visit Taiwan during winter/summer vacation. I was able to adapt both American and Taiwanese culture. I love the people here, many American friend of mine would say Taiwan is truly a beautiful place to live and work.

  • Communicate and understanding different cultures and meeting new people.
  • Enthusiastic with creativities, and likes to work with team to build relationship and to achieve goals.
  • Research, interact and analysis user interface and user experience.
